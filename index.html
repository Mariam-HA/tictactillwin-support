FAQs:

How do I play TicTacTillWin?

To play TicTacTillWin, follow these steps:

  1. Start the game by tapping on the 'New Game' button.
  2. Player 'X' makes the first move by tapping on an empty square in the 3x3 grid.
  3. Players alternate turns, tapping on empty squares to place their marks ('X' or 'O').
  4. Each player can only have three marks on the board at a time. When a fourth mark is placed, the oldest mark disappears.
  5. The game continues until one player forms a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line with their marks, winning the game.
