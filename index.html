FAQs:

How do I play online with a friend?

To play TicTacTillWin online with a friend, follow these steps:

  1. One player creates a room by tapping on 'Create A Room' and sharing the room code with the friend.
  2. The other player joins the room by tapping on 'Enter A Room' and entering the shared room code.
  3. Both players will be assigned marks ('X' or 'O') and can start playing by tapping on empty squares.

What happens if a player quits the game?

If a player quits the game, both players will be navigated back to the home screen. You can start a new game or create/join another room to play again.

How do I report a bug or issue?

If you encounter a bug or issue while using TicTacTillWin, please follow these steps:

  1. Take a screenshot of the issue, if possible.
  2. Write a brief description of the problem, including steps to reproduce it.
  3. Email the details to our support team at murmur0909@gmail.com.

We appreciate your feedback and will work to resolve any issues as quickly as possible.
